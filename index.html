Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the BAC estimation?

Bar Buddy uses scientifically validated formulas to estimate your BAC based on your weight, gender, drinks consumed, and time elapsed. While our calculations are highly accurate, they are estimates only and shouldn't be used to determine if you're legally able to drive. Always err on the side of caution.

Is there a subscription fee after the initial purchase?

No, Bar Buddy uses a one-time purchase model. Pay once and enjoy all features forever, including future updates.

Can I use Bar Buddy with my Apple Watch?

Yes! Bar Buddy features full Apple Watch integration. You can quickly log drinks from your watch, view your current BAC, and see your estimated time until sober. We even offer watch complications to keep this information visible at a glance.

Which devices are compatible with Bar Buddy?

Bar Buddy is currently available for iOS devices running iOS 18.2 or later, with full Apple Watch integration. We're actively working on an Android version, which we plan to release in the future.

How does Bar Buddy handle my personal data?

Your privacy is important to us. Bar Buddy stores your data locally on your device. Your drinking history and personal information never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to share it. Location data is only used when you activate safety features like rideshare integration or status sharing.
